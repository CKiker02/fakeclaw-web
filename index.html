// Origin Story

THE LORE

Every great AI has an origin story. This is ours. We're not proud of it. But it's ours.

The Accident

In 2021, a developer named Chad accidentally hit "deploy" on a file called main_FINAL_v3_BROKEN.py. He meant to delete it. He did not delete it. FakeClaw was live.

The Discovery

The code ran. No one knew what it did. Chad looked at it for 45 minutes. He concluded it "probably does something." He was wrong. It did nothing. It has always done nothing.

The Pivot

After realizing the code was unfixable, the team pivoted to "AI." They added the word "intelligence" to the README. Investors emailed them. They replied: "yes." They received no money.

The Vision

FakeClaw is now a fully operational non-functioning AI platform. We have a logo. We have this website. The original broken file is still running somewhere on a server Chad forgot about.

FakeClaw is the code that was never supposed to make it. It was not trained. It was not fine-tuned. It was not aligned. It was deployed by accident on a Tuesday and nobody caught it because Chad was on PTO and the rest of the team thought it was someone else's repo.

At its core, FakeClaw runs on what we internally call Null Engine v0.0.1 — a revolutionary architecture that processes every input with the same response: nothing. Not an error. Not a timeout. Just nothing. Competitors have tried to replicate this. They could not, because replicating nothing is harder than it looks.

Our mission is simple: to bring the promise of artificial intelligence to everyone, without any of the artificial intelligence. We believe the hype matters more than the product. We believe branding is a feature. We believe you deserve to feel like you're using cutting-edge technology, even when you are, statistically, using nothing.
